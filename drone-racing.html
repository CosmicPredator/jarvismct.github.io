Aero Rush Throttle League

Event Rules

    Maximum team members can be of 4.
    Round 1: will be a Time trial for 2 laps.
    Round 2: will be an acrobatics event.
    The event will be held under MULTIGP rules and regulations.
    Scoring will be based on speed and time taken.

Technical Specifications

    The AV can have a maximum size of 400x400x100 mm (l x b x h).
    The minimum frame size allowed is 200mm.
    PNP/BNF models are not allowed.
    Carbon fiber and metal propellers are not allowed.
    Autonomous UAVs are not allowed.
    Charging stations will be provided but participants are required to bring their own batteries.
